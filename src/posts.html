New Years Resolutions

What are my New Year’s resolutions?

I have none.

Why?

New Year’s resolutions are thought to be a new beginning to a new you. While they work for a few, many of the times, research shows people give up on New Year’s resolutions within the first two weeks.

They are usually something that are overhyped and set up to fail. Many want to change but are not in the position, nor mindset to do so.

Set resolutions during the week. During the month. Set them when you are ready to change something, not just because society says you should.

Motivation or Inspiration?

I had always used the terms interchangeably, but after some thinking I realized there is a significant difference between the two.

When I think of motivation, I think of a mood that you are in. You felt good today and felt motivated to do something.

Thinking of inspiration, it's more of a mindset you are in. You saw someone do something great and you want to be like them.

To me, it's clear that inspiration is the bigger kicker here. Mindset is much more powerful (and important) component than a mood. Inspiration can allow you to stay consistently working at something, because there is an end goal you have in mind.

Moral of the story here? Find some inspiration and get to work.
